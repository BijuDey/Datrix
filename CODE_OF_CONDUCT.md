# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus discussions on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or hateful language
- Personal attacks or discriminatory remarks
- Deliberate disruption or bad-faith participation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.

Actions may include:

- Warning
- Temporary restriction
- Permanent ban from participation

## Reporting

Report incidents privately to project maintainers with:

- What happened
- Links or evidence
- Impact and context

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with care.
